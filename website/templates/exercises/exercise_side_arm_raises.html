Instructions

  1. Raise both arms, to the side of the body to shoulder level
  2. Keep shoulder blades retracted and depressed throughout the exercise.
  3. Do not allow the low-back to arch. Hold.
  4. Return arms slowly back to the side of the body and repeat
Tips for posture

Never lock the elbow joint while doing this exercise. You have to do an almost “T” and hold.
