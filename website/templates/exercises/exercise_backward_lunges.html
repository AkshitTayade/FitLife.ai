Instructions

  1. Stand upright, with your hands at your side. Take a large step backward with your left foot.
  2. Lower your hips so that your right thigh becomes parallel to the floor. Your left knee should be bent at a 90-degree angle and pointing toward the floor with your left heel lifted.
  3. Return to standing by pressing your right heel into the floor and bringing your left leg forward to complete one rep.
  4. Alternate legs, and step back with right leg.
Tips for posture

Ensure the toes and knee of your back leg are facing the same direction throughout the entire move.
